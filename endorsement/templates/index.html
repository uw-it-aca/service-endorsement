This tool allows you to manage non-employees' ability to access UW Office 365 and/or UW G Suite (Google). Use this to vouch that someone who has a UW NetID is eligible to use these services because of an established working relationship with UW. This tool does not allow you to provision services to shared UW NetIDs at this time.

You will need to know the UW NetID of each person to whom you are provisioning services, as well as a valid email address at which they can receive email.
